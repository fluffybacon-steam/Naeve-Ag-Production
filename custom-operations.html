Custom Operations

Planting

We will prepare your soil and plant your crop. We plant our crops in 30‚Äù rows and our soybeans in 20" rows for better sunlight utilization which generally translates to high yields. We use Global Position Satellite (GPS) and auto-steer technology to accurately plant and map the location of each variety.

Custom Land Rolling

We can roll your soil before or after planting for better seed to soil contact and to assist and enhance the planting and harvesting operations.

Custom Spraying

We have two high clearance sprayers with 120' Boom, GPS guidance and auto shut-off technology for accurate and efficient spraying. We spray to effectively and economically control yield robbing weeds, insects and funguses.

Detasseling (Roller Puller)

Brent has a 2018 D400 detasseling machine that mechanically removes the tassels from the female seed corn plants.

Seed Corn Harvest

We have two Oxbo seed corn harvesters with 12-row John Deere corn heads specifically designed for the harvest of seed corn. These machines are designed to gently harvest the high moisture, whole corn ear, in the husk for improved seed quality.

Corn/Soybean Harvest

We can combine your corn and/or soybeans and ensure the maximum yield is harvested with excellent seed quality. We have GPS Yield Monitor technology in our combine that tracts the varieties and the yields and can provide instant yield data and maps.

Tillage

We have a complete line of field-cultivator, disk, disk-ripper or in-line ripper.

Tiling

We design and install field drainage tile with a backhoe and a GPS depth-controlled plow. After installation, we provide a GPS map to assist in the future location of the tile lines. We can also fix existing drainage issues and complete surface-drainage.
