Custom Operations

Planting

We will prepare your soil and plant your crop. We plant all of our crops in 20‚Äù rows for better sunlight utilization which generally translates to high yields. We use Global Position Satellite (GPS) and auto-steer technology to accurately plant and map the location of each variety.

Custom Land Rolling

We can roll your soil before or after planting for better seed to soil contact and to assist and enhance the planting and harvesting operations.

Custom Spraying

We have two high clearance sprayers with 105' booms and Reichardt Guidance technology for accurate and efficient spraying. We spray to effectively and economically control yield robbing weeds, insects and funguses.

Detasseling (Roller Puller)

Brent has a 2011 Hagie high-clearance detasseling machine that mechanically removes the tassels from the female seed corn plants.

Seed Corn Harvest

We have two Oxbo seed corn harvesters with 12-row John Deere corn heads specifically designed for the harvest of seed corn. These machines are designed to gently harvest the high moisture, whole corn ear, in the husk for improved seed quality.

Corn/Soybean Harvest

We can combine your corn and/or soybeans and ensure the maximum yield is harvested with excellent seed quality. We have GPSOLYMPUS DIGITAL CAMERA technology in our combine that tracts the varieties and the yields and can provide instant yield data and maps.

Tillage

We can complete fall or spring tillage with a complete line of disk, disk-ripper or in-line ripper.

Tiling

We design and install field drainage tile with a backhoe and a laser depth-controlled plow. After installation, we provide a GPS map to assist in the future location of the tile lines. We can also fix existing drainage issues and complete surface-drainage.
